  • the specifics
  • Neutral characters, or characters with undeclared stances (i.e. most of the X-Men, and The Thing) are not represented. Also, characters that appeared in Civil War #1, but before the Registration Act was passed were not included.
  • This project would have just sat around gathering moss if not for friends like Matthew Davis John Otis, and Beau Tidwell (thanks you guys!)
  • technological
  • This google spreadsheet powers the entire page.
  • Balance Media's tabletop.js hooks the spreadsheet into my project. It's the secret sauce.
  • All character data for this project comes from the marvel comics site and the comicvine API.
  • Issue data and reading order came from good old fashioned interneting (meaning it could be totally wrong.) The bulk of it came from here and here.
